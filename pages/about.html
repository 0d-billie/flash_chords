About Flash Chords

Flash chords is an application designed to help learn guitar chords in their various positions. It was born out of my realising that I don't know many real chords, and should learn some.

More exciting features coming soon!

Technologies Used:

  • jQuery
  • SVG.js
  • Chord DB
  • Bootstrap
  • Font Awesome
